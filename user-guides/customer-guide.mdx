## Overview

Welcome to ScooterShare! This guide will help you navigate the platform, book scooters, manage your account balance, and more.

## Getting Started

### Viewing Available Scooters

Browse all available scooters in your area:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Available Scooters">
    Navigate to `/view_available_scooters` to see all scooters with status "Available"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Scooter Details">
    Each scooter listing shows:
    - **ScooterID**: Unique identifier
    - **Make**: Scooter manufacturer
    - **Colour**: Scooter color
    - **Location**: Current location
    - **RemainingPower**: Battery percentage
    - **CostPerTime**: Rental cost per time unit
    - **Status**: Availability status
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
The system fetches data from the `/available_scooters` API endpoint (customer.py:240)
</Info>

## Booking Scooters

### How to Book a Scooter

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Scooter">
    Choose an available scooter from the list and note its ScooterID
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Initiate Booking">
    Click the book button or navigate to `/book_scooter_page/<scooter_id>`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Booking">
    The system will:
    - Update scooter status to "Occupying"
    - Create a booking record with status "Active"
    - Set lock status to "Locked"
    - Award you 100 ranking points
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Booking creates an entry in the bookings table and automatically awards 100 points to your ranking (customer.py:90-154)
</Note>

### Managing Your Bookings

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="View Bookings" icon="list">
    Access `/customer_view_bookings` to see all your booking history including:
    - Booking ID
    - Scooter ID
    - Booking time
    - Booking status (Active/Completed/Cancelled)
    - Lock status (Locked/Unlocked)
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Cancel Booking" icon="xmark">
    Navigate to `/customer_cancel_booking/<booking_id>` to cancel an active booking. This will:
    - Update booking status to "Cancelled"
    - Set scooter status back to "Available"
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
View your bookings via the `/customer_bookings/<user_id>` endpoint (customer.py:165)
</Info>

## Using Scooters

### Lock and Unlock Features

#### Unlocking a Scooter

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Lock/Unlock Page">
    Navigate to `/customer_lock_unlock` to view your active bookings
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Account Balance">
    Ensure you have sufficient funds (at least equal to CostPerTime)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Unlock Scooter">
    Click unlock for your booked scooter at `/customer_unlock_scooter/<scooter_id>/<booking_id>`
    
    The system verifies your balance before unlocking
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
You cannot unlock a scooter if your available balance is less than the CostPerTime (customer.py:574-604)
</Warning>

#### Locking a Scooter

When you're done using the scooter:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Provide New Location">
    Enter the location where you're parking the scooter
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Lock the Scooter">
    Navigate to `/customer_lock_scooter/<scooter_id>/<booking_id>?newLocation=<location>`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Automatic Processing">
    The system will:
    - Deduct the CostPerTime from your balance
    - Update lock status to "Locked"
    - Update booking status to "Completed"
    - Set scooter status to "Available"
    - Update scooter location
    - Create a usage record
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Locking a scooter automatically creates a usage entry with 10 minutes time and 10km distance (customer.py:606-670)
</Info>

## Account Management

### Viewing Your Balance

Check your account balance:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Balance Page">
    Navigate to `/customer_view_balance` to view your current available money
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Balance">
    Your balance is fetched from the `/customer_balance_data/<user_id>` endpoint
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Topping Up Your Balance

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Top-Up">
    Access the balance page at `/customer_view_balance`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Amount">
    Submit a form with the desired `topup_amount`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Process Payment">
    POST to `/update_user_money` which will:
    - Retrieve your current balance
    - Add the top-up amount
    - Update your account
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Top-up amounts are added to your existing balance via the `/update_user_money` endpoint (customer.py:341-373)
</Note>

## Usage History

Track your scooter usage over time:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Usage History">
    Navigate to `/customer_usage_history_render`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Records">
    See all your past scooter usage including:
    - Usage ID
    - Scooter ID
    - Time used (in minutes)
    - Distance covered (in km)
    - Usage date
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Usage history is retrieved from `/customer_usage_history/<user_id>` (customer.py:279)
</Info>

## Reviews and Ratings

### Adding a Review

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Scooter">
    Navigate to `/add_reviews/<scooter_id>` for the scooter you want to review
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill Review Form">
    Provide:
    - User ID (auto-populated from session)
    - Scooter ID
    - Review text
    - Rating (numeric value)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Review">
    POST to `/add_review` to save your review
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Viewing Reviews

See all scooter reviews at `/customer_review` which displays:
- Review ID
- User information
- Scooter information
- Review text
- Rating

<Info>
Reviews are stored in the reviews table and can be viewed via `/get_review` (customer.py:402-430)
</Info>

## Reporting Issues

### Report a Faulty Scooter

If you encounter a problem with a scooter:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Report Page">
    Navigate to `/customer_report_scooter/<scooter_id>`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Report">
    The form will be pre-populated with scooter details and your user ID
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Repair Request">
    Submit to `/add_repair/1` which will:
    - Change scooter status to "To Be Repaired"
    - Create a repair entry with status "In Progress"
    - Notify all engineers via email
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Once a scooter is reported, its status changes to "To Be Repaired" and it becomes unavailable for booking (admin.py:416)
</Warning>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens when I book a scooter?">
    When you book a scooter:
    1. The scooter status changes to "Occupying"
    2. A booking record is created with status "Active" and lock status "Locked"
    3. You receive 100 ranking points
    4. The scooter is reserved for your use
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I unlock a scooter without sufficient balance?">
    No, the system checks your available balance against the scooter's CostPerTime. You must have at least the CostPerTime amount in your account to unlock a scooter. If you don't have enough funds, you'll receive an error message prompting you to top up your account.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How is usage tracked?">
    When you lock a scooter after use, the system automatically records:
    - Scooter ID
    - User ID
    - Time used (currently set to 10 minutes)
    - Distance covered (currently set to 10 km)
    - Usage date
    
    This creates a usage history entry that you can view later.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens when I cancel a booking?">
    Canceling a booking:
    1. Updates the booking status to "Cancelled"
    2. Changes the scooter status back to "Available"
    3. Makes the scooter available for other customers to book
    
    Note: Cancellation does not affect your ranking points.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I see my active bookings?">
    Navigate to `/customer_view_bookings` to see all your bookings, including:
    - Active bookings (currently in use)
    - Completed bookings (past rides)
    - Cancelled bookings
    
    Each booking shows the scooter ID, booking time, status, and lock status.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I review any scooter?">
    Yes, you can add reviews for any scooter by navigating to `/add_reviews/<scooter_id>`. Your review should include a text description and a numeric rating. All reviews are visible on the customer review page.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
